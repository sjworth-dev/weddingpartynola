The Problem

Hotel Blocks Are Broken

You book a hotel block for the wedding party. Everyone's on different floors. Half the rooms overlook the parking lot. There's nowhere to hang out together except the lobby bar.

The rehearsal dinner ends and everyone goes to their separate rooms. The morning of the wedding, the bridesmaids are crammed into one room trying to do hair and makeup.

This isn't how wedding weekends should feel.

The Private Property Experience

  • Quality time before and after events
